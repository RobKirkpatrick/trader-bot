STRATEGY OVERVIEW
=================

Core Edge:
  NWS forecasts are scientifically calibrated
  Kalshi weather markets are priced by retail traders
  Gap between forecast and price = tradable edge

Entry Rule:
  (NWS probability - Kalshi ask price) > MIN_EDGE (10 cents)
  AND market resolves 0.5-7 days from now
  AND bid-ask spread < 10 cents

Position Sizing:
  Scales from $1-$20 based on edge strength
  Larger edges → larger positions
  Capital-efficient, risk-adjusted

Exit Logic:
  Normal: Hold to market resolution
  Early: Exit if NWS shifts >15 cents against position

Why It Works:
  Scientific forecast vs. retail opinion
  Free, public, accurate NWS API
  Clear signal without directional bet
  Sustainable, non-crowded edge
